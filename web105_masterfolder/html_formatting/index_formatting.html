The Dark Knight Trilogy

Plot Summary

The Dark Knight Trilogy is about Bruce Wayne growing from a young boy, to a true hero. It details him fighting the criminal scum of Gotham.

The first movie The Dark Knight Begins Batman Begins has Batman face off against the mafia, The League of Shadows, and Scarcrow. Batman famously said I'm Batman. when he first appeared

The second movie in the trilogy introduces the Joker and him and Batman do battle for Gotham city.

Alfred, Batman's faithful butler, described Joker's personality best when he said:

...some men aren't looking for anything logical, like money. They can't be bought, bullied, reasoned, or negotiated with. Some men just want to watch the world burn. -Alfred Pennyworth

The third movie has Batman face off against his greatest opponent Bane. While Bane tries to follow the legacy of the League of Shadows, and destroy Gotham.

About the Director

The trilogy was directed by Christopher Nolan. He is a very famous director, and has many famous and highly praised movies. A list of his movies can be found here: Christopher Nolan Filmography
